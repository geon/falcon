You decide to Thinkstrike the stocky Rigellian as he is nearest to the clump of trees that disguise Falcon's Wing. You hurl a blast of mental energy at him. There is a grunt of pain and he throws his head back and staggers like a wounded elephant. The other Rigellian does not hesitate; he twists aside and, his arm a blur, seems to pass the twin-bladed sword across the stocky Rigellian's thigh, opening up a deep wound. He follows up with a mighty axe blow, and the stocky Rigellian is hurled backwards. Suddenly he disappears into a tree. To your horror you realise he has fallen into the hologram disguising Falcon's Wing! The taller Rigellian stands stock still in utter amazement. You must act quickly.
